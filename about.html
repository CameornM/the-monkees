Band Members

Davy Jones

Born in December 1945, Dany was best known for lead vocals on many of The Monkees records. Jones also used to play the Bass when Tork was on the keyboard.

Michael Nesmith

Born in December 1942, he was known as the guy with "a wool hat" for wearing it to his audition, Michael used a 12-string electric guitar as part of The Monkees

Peter Tork

Born in February 1942, Peter was well know for having a very diverse skill set and played keyboards, bass guitar, banjo and other instruments in their recordings.

Micky Dolenz

Born in December 1942, Micky was the drummer and one of the lead vocalist for the band. He learned to play the drums right-handed and left-footed because of a leg disease called Perthes making his right leg weak.
